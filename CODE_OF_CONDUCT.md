# Code of Conduct

## Our standards
We are committed to a respectful, inclusive, and harassment-free environment.

Examples of expected behavior:
- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent and give helpful feedback.
- Respect differing viewpoints and experiences.

Unacceptable behavior includes:
- Harassment, discrimination, or abuse.
- Trolling, personal attacks, or derogatory comments.
- Publishing private information without consent.

## Scope
This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces, including issues, pull requests, discussions, and community chats related to the project.

## Reporting and enforcement
If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it to:
- phucnguyen20031976@gmail.com
- lonelycoder0710@gmail.com

Reports will be handled confidentially. Maintainers may take any action they deem appropriate, including content removal, warnings, temporary bans, or permanent bans.

## Enforcement guidelines
- Warning: a private, written warning.
- Temporary ban: a time-limited ban from interaction.
- Permanent ban: removal of access to project spaces.
